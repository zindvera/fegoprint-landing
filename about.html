About FegoPrint

For Customers For Seller

For Customers

FegoPrint is a visual preview website that helps you understand how a product will actually look before you decide to buy it on an external store.


For wall art

Use a live camera preview to place the wall art on your own wall and adjust the position.

View the product in different room scenes (such as bedroom or living room) to see how it fits various interiors.

Change the wall colour behind the frame to see how it looks on light, dark or coloured walls.


For stickers

Use a live camera preview to position the sticker on real objects around you and check size and placement.

See the sticker on different device types and colours, such as laptops, tablets or other surfaces.


Buying the product

After exploring these previews, you click the “Buy” button and complete the purchase on an external store like Amazon, Redbubble or another seller website.


Other products (future extension)

Right now FegoPrint focuses on wall art, stickers and wall decor, but the same preview tools are built to support more product types in the future.

Any product that benefits from clearer visual presentation in different environments or on different devices can be added later, using a mix of static mockups, colour options and live camera previews.

For Seller (Product Owners)

FegoPrint is a presentation tool that helps you show how your products will look in a customer's chosen environment, so customers can see this before they visit your external store to buy.


For wall art

Let customers use a live camera preview to place your artwork on their own wall and move it around.

Present each wall art product in multiple room scenes to match different interior styles and moods.

Offer wall colour options so visitors can test how your wall art looks on light, dark or coloured walls.


For stickers

Allow customers to use a live camera preview to position your sticker on their own items and judge size, placement and overall fit.

Display your stickers on different device types and colours, such as laptops, tablets or other objects, so customers can see how they match real devices.


External “Buy” button

Each product page on FegoPrint includes a “Buy” button that links directly to your existing store, such as Amazon, Redbubble or your own website.

FegoPrint does not handle checkout or shipping; it focuses on improving how your products are visually presented before purchase.


Other products (future extension)

At present FegoPrint focuses on wall art, stickers and wall decor, but the same system is designed to support more product categories over time.

You will be able to attach your products to new mockup types and visual options whenever additional product categories are added.
