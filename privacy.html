Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FegoPrint handles information when you use the website and its preview features.

1. What FegoPrint does

FegoPrint is a visual preview website that lets you see how products such as wall art, stickers and wall decor could look in different environments before you buy them on external stores.

2. Camera and preview features

Some previews use your device camera so you can place a product on your own wall or objects.

  • The camera is used only to generate the live preview on your screen.
  • FegoPrint does not record, store or upload your camera feed or preview images.
  • You can stop camera access at any time using your browser or device settings.

3. Cookies and local storage

FegoPrint uses minimal cookies or similar storage.

  • A small cookie or local storage entry may be used to remember whether you have already seen a camera preview popup, so the same message is not shown repeatedly.
  • This information does not identify you personally and is not used for advertising or tracking across other websites.

4. External stores and links

Product pages on FegoPrint include “Buy” buttons that link to external stores such as Amazon, Redbubble or other seller websites.

  • When you click these links you leave FegoPrint and visit a third‑party website.
  • Those websites have their own privacy practices, which apply to any information you provide there.

5. Information you choose to share

If you contact FegoPrint by email, the email address and the information you send are used only to reply to your request and to provide support.

6. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The “Privacy Policy” heading above shows the page you are currently viewing; when the text changes, the updated version will apply from the date it is published here.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us using the email address shown on the Contact page.
